How to Play Weezle!
(paraphrased from the Wordle website)
  1. Type in your 5-letter word guess and hit enter.
  2. The letters of the word will change color based on their placement
    1. TBA: The letter is in the word, however in the incorrect spot.
    2. TBA: The letter is in the word, and the correct spot!
    3. TBA: The letter is not in the word at all.
  3. You have six (6) tries to solve the Weezle!
